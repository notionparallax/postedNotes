What we do…

A handwritten letter is a beautiful thing, which we don't see much anymore. Maybe you don't have a stamp or forget to go to the post office - something always gets in the way of mailing that letter.

If this sounds like you then Posted Notes is here to help.

We take your text and turn it into a beautiful, handwritten note. We put a stamp on it and post it.

There is no sign up and each letter costs $5 including delivery. We've kept it really simple so all you need to think about are the words.

We will write every letter by hand on A5 paper and put it into a matching envelope. We'll write the address on the front and pop on a stamp. We will post the letter in the recipient's country.

How…

1

2

3

Where…

Pro Tips

If you begin a line with a > we'll right align it. This is handy for formatting addresses.

If you put a line of text in between angle brackets:
> your text here <
then we'll indent it both sides, and if possible, centre it.

If you run your text through the Hemmingway App it almost always comes out better for it!

The Rules

We'd love to spend all day writing thank you cards for grandmas, love letters, anonymous high five letters and other feel good things. We don't mind if you get us to write letters to your landlord or pleas for the library to let you off your fines. We'll even politely fire all your staff or tell someone how terribly rude they are. We won't send death threats or Ransom notes.

Privacy

We promise we won't read your letters out loud or let anyone else do that either. They go directly to one of our scribes and then once they are in the letterbox nobody will see them again. The only place your data gets stored is with paypal, and their security makes most banks look lax!
