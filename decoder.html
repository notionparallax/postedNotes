Procedure

While we're small this is going to rely on some reasonably low-tech solutions. Here's my attempt at codifying the procedure, but I'd love some feedback in how you think this should be changed to make it more efficient.

  1. Someone writes a letter. They fill in the address and the message, as well as ticking the Ts&Cs box. (This will make it pass the form validation.)
  2. They pay with Paypal
  3. Paypal then sends me their receipt along with all the data we need.
  4. My inbox automatically forwards the receipt to all of you (the writers).
  5. Someone then does a reply allto accept the job and let the others know not to do any unnecessary writing. You should check the region before accepting as it only really makes sense to write for your region. (You can tell the region by looking at the address at the end of the code block; it will usually not be broken up.)
  6. The person that accepted the job then writes the letter.
    • Using the pen specified
    • Using one side of the paper only
    • Neatly and observing the formatting cues > centred <and > right aligned
  7. To get the letter properly formatted, take the block of text (click the button to see the important bit outlined in red)
  8. Use a first class stamp
  9. Once the letter is written, the address is written and the stamp is put on then take a photo or scan the letter (all pages) and the envelope. A mobile phone photo is plenty.
  10. Post the letter as soon as possible
  11. Share the picture of the letter with me (Ben)
  12. [coming soon: send the picture directly to the customer using an email template (once I've made it).]
